Hello and welcome! :) Lets get your Foundry VTT instance online!

First, lets get to your profile.

  1. Open FoundryVTT
  2. Log in with your account.
  3. Click the profile link in the upper right hand corner.

Now lets get the timed link!

  1. Once on your profile click Purchased Licenses.
  2. (OPTIONAL) Change the version drop down.
  3. Select Linux/NodeJS
  4. Click Timed URL

Time to paste the link and click submit!

Successfully submitted FoundryVTT url!
